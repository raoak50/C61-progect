About

Looney Tunes cartoons featuring Bugs Bunny make me laugh. He appeals to my rebel side. He is very patriotic. He raised more money via War Bonds than anyone else during WWII. He is multi-talented. He is smart, athletic, musically inclined, and quick witted. He uses "stragedy" to win out over his opponents. He has stood the test of time. Over 170 cartoons feature Bugs Bunny and he has been very popular for over 80 years. View Cartoon List He is arguably the most popular cartoon character of all time! He is always optimistic about the outcome of his conflicts. He eventually comes out on top. He is a sucker for pretty blondes (e.g. Honey Bunny, Daisy Lou, and Lola Bunny).
